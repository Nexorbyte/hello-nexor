Make a difference
not coffee

Making the right career choice after graduating is one of the most important decisions you’ll make. At Propel Tech we offer a number of fantastic early career programmes that will allow you to make tech happen from day one.

Our graduate and summer internship opportunities aim to provide the next generation of full stack software developers with a springboard to develop their technical, behavioural and commercial skills.

Technologies

At Propel Tech we pride ourselves on having highly skilled teams, able to harness multiple software platforms and languages. This advanced technical knowledge and experience is key to our success, but we also know that software experts need to start somewhere.

Our early career programmes offer a unique opportunity to apply cutting-edge tools and technologies, and our teams will mentor and help you to elevate your coding skills.

Graduates

As a graduate software developer at Propel Tech you’ll be treated as an individual, with the flexibility to personalise your graduate journey.

You'll be given the opportunity to develop new bespoke systems using leading edge development technologies, as well as maintain and enhance existing client systems.

And there’s no lengthy training bootcamp to complete first, we know you’re eager to get going. Following our two-week onboarding programme, you’ll be straight in to learning on the job, meaning you’ll be working on client systems and projects from the start.

As well as developing your behavioural and commercial skills, as a Propel Tech graduate software developer you'll learn about:

  • Building advanced systems using technologies such as React, Nativescript, CSS and Bootstrap
  • Database design/SQL
  • Object oriented coding
  • MVC frameworks like Laravel web applications
  • JavaScript development
  • GIT/Version control
  • Docker and containerised deployment
  • Server management

Our next application window opens October 2023

Summer Internships

The Propel Tech Software Developer Intern Programme provides students with experience and understanding of what it means to work in software development, before you reach the end of your studies and begin applying for permanent positions.

Over the eight week programme you’ll be given the opportunity to try out developing systems using development technologies, such as the Laravel PHP framework, using Bootstrap as a frontend CSS framework.

You will work one on one with an experienced developer, who will act as your mentor and provide advice and guidance as you progress through a simulated client project, from understanding the clients brief right through to presenting your final system.

As you move through the project you will have the chance to meet people from various areas of the business. You will discuss coding aspects with developers, evaluate your work alongside the testing team and deploy your code to the cloud whilst working with infrastructure experts.

Want to know more? Get a first hand view from our recent intern Martin!

And you won’t just be developing your technical skills; you’ll also learn about the commercial and behavioural skills which will be essential for your future career in software development.

Our next application window opens October 2023

Flexible from the start

We’re a small but growing business with a bold ambition to move all businesses forward with the right technology, but we couldn’t do this without our talented team members, so we know it’s important to support them both professionally and personally.

We offer flexible and individual working arrangements to all team member’s; this covers not only where you work but your hours and your development. As a business we look to help our team to move forward with their goals, this includes a role built around you - with a focus on helping you progress your career whilst enjoying your current role!

Find out more about what it's like to work at Propel Tech

Build connections

All our early careers programmes give you the opportunity to be part of a supportive team, where you'll be mentored by your team leader and colleagues.

You’ll also have plenty of opportunity to get to know colleagues across all of our development, testing and project management teams, with our regular events and activities from lunchtime pizza parties to pool & darts tournaments and social events.

And it’s not just internal connections you’ll build, we won’t hide you away, you’ll liaise with clients directly, working as their expert technology partner to understand their business software and application needs.
