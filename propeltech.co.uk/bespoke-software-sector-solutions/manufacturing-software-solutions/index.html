Transforming manufacturing with bespoke software development

Our mission is to make tech possible and empower manufacturing businesses by developing intuitive, scalable, and secure software solutions that address sector-specific challenges such as production management, inventory control, supply chain logistics, and machine integration.

We build software that seamlessly integrates with existing systems, ensuring minimal disruption and maximum impact.

Our manufacturing-focused bespoke software services

We offer a full suite of bespoke software development, and modernisation services tailored to manufacturing, whether it's custom APIs, real-time interfaces, systems modernisation, team augmentation or cloud solutions, our services are built to accommodate hardware changes, platform migrations and systems upgrades, improving compatibility and performance throughout the entire software and manufacturing lifecycle. Services we offer include:

Manufacturing process automation

Our team can work to reduce manual intervention and improve productivity by automating repetitive tasks such as production scheduling, quality control, and equipment monitoring, allowing your team to focus on more strategic areas of work.

Inventory and supply chain management systems

We can work with you to optimise your inventory management and supply chain system, we offer bespoke software solutions for example we can provide real-time tracking, inventory forecasting, and automated order processing.

Production monitoring and reporting

Gain valuable insights into your production line performance with bespoke-built dashboards, KPI monitoring, and data-driven reporting systems improvement.

Systems modernisation and productivity integrations

We work with manufacturing companies to improve legacy systems, we enhance and modernise your existing systems by updating, migrating and integrating them with bespoke software that brings new functionality, improving efficiency, communication and data flow across departments and manufacturing systems.

Custom cloud and IoT solutions

We improve, modernise and develop IoT-enabled systems that connect your manufacturing process, providing transparency and improved real-time data collection and analysis to improve operational efficiency. For example, our bespoke software development services include creating flexible communication protocols for hardware in its prototype stage, ensuring seamless integration and functionality regardless of the final product iteration.

Manufacturing systems testing and testing automation

Our software testing experts are highly experienced and highly trained across all forms of manufacturing systems testing. Testing is a vital process for manufacturing businesses, faulty software or systems issues could lead to delays in production. It can also cause defects in final products or even safety issues.
